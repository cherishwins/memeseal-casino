Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TON wallet, we may access your public wallet address. We do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary for the application to function.

2. How We Use Information

  • To enable wallet-based authentication
  • To track game statistics and leaderboards
  • To improve the application

3. Blockchain Transparency

All transactions on the TON blockchain are public by nature. Your wallet address and transaction history are visible on-chain.

4. Third-Party Services

We use TON Connect for wallet integration. Please review TON Connect's privacy practices.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via Telegram.
